About Me

A Trusted Partner for Your Financial Journey

I am an independent, AMFI-registered Mutual Fund Distributor dedicated to helping individuals and families grow their wealth through disciplined, goal-based investing. No corporate agenda, no product quotas — just honest guidance aligned entirely with your interests.

Currently guiding ₹50,000+ in monthly SIP commitments and managing ₹3 Lakhs+ in assets across a growing, trust-first client base. Six months into this practice — and backed by 5 years of hands-on experience in the stock market and trading.

Every client relationship begins with a conversation, not a proposal. I take the time to understand where you are, where you want to go, and then build a practical, personalised investment roadmap to get you there.

Future plans include offering Portfolio Management Services (PMS) under Shastri Financial Services as the practice continues to grow.
